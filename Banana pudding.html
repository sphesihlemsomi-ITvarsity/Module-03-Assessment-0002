Banana Pudding

Banana pudding is a dessert generally consisting of layers of sweet vanilla flavored custard, cookies and sliced fresh bananas placed in a dish and served, topped with whipped cream or meringue. It is commonly associated with Southern American cuisine, but it can be found around the country.
